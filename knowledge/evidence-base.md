## Confidence Levels

- **HIGH:** Multiple credible sources agree, recent data (2024-2026), quantifiable
- **MEDIUM:** Single credible source, or data from 2022-2023, or self-reported metrics
- **LOW:** Anecdotal, practitioner opinion, or extrapolated from limited data
